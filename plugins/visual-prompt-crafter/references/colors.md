## Color Terms

- High contrast (light vs dark)
- Low contrast (similar values)
- Desaturated (muted)
- Hyper saturated (vivid)
- Complementary (opposite on wheel)
- Analogous (adjacent on wheel)
- Monochrome (one hue)
- Black and white
- Sepia (brown tint)
